Our Story

Jose Andres Guzman founded "Mocans" with the goal of bringing the taste of San Victor, Moca to the Dominican cigar market. He began by growing the highest quality tobacco on his farmland. After cultivating the tobacco leafs his master cigar makers went on to create blends that capture the unique flavor profile of San Victor, Moca.

Cigars

The quality of the tobacco used in Mocans cigars as well as the construction and consistency paired with the flavor profile make it an unforgetable experience.

Fronto Leaf

Our fronto leafs have a rich bold flavor and a smooth texture. They are used to roll cigars or cigarettes. The leaf burns evenly and slowly, while you enjoy the flavor and aroma.
